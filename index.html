What documents should I prepare for the Seller Account registration?

Before registering for a papmall® Seller Account, you should prepare some documents for us to get a clear understanding of your products and services.

  • Personal Documents:
    • Identification Documents: your Identity Card or Passport.
    • Proof of Address: Utility Bill (Water Bill, Electricity Bill, Landline Telephone Bill, etc.), or the latest Bank Statement, Bank Reference.
If you register under a Company, Agency, or Business, papmall® will need a full set of your business documents:
  • Company Documents:

How can I get paid when I sell my products and services to Buyers?

After an order is complete, earnings will automatically be directed to your Seller Account. You can withdraw the funds to your registered bank accounts, or your PayPal accounts once you have connected them to papmall®.

papmall® Protect is papmall® special program made to prevent scams and safeguard Sellers and Buyers from fraud transactions. Until the fulfillment is complete, papmall® is going to hold the order payment in escrow, and only releasing it to Sellers once Buyers have confirmed receipt of the order or if the deadline for confirmation has passed. For more information, visit papmall® Terms & Conditions and Refund & Return Policy. For further assistance, please contact the papmall® support team via Chat Box or support@papmall.com.
